Dope Focus

Andrew Zap

A collaboration between Brooklyn-based singer-songwriter Andrew Zap and Kalamazoo-based producer Joel PIxley-Fink. What does it sound like when big city and small town come together? In one magical evening, former band mates Andrew “Zap” Zapanta, a Brooklyn-based musician and visual artist, and Joel Pixley-Fink, bassist, producer, multi-instrumentalist, reconnected in the small town of Kalamazoo, MI while Andrew was on his Late Night/Lo-Fi national tour. In a creative burst of just a few hours they recorded and finished “Monday Morning.” This made them think they were onto something special and inspired them to create an EP’s worth of material. Two years later, after countless trips between Kalamazoo and Brooklyn, their debut collaborative record, Dope Focus, is ready for the world. The single “Monday Morning” will be released in November along with a music video. The full EP will be released in February, 2017. The music blends hip-hop, soul, and traditional songwriting craft into a raw, authentic new sound. A limited edition blue vinyl will be available on Pledge Music, along with CDs, T-Shirts and more. Zapanta and Pixley-Fink are currently booking a national tour for Summer 2017 in support of Dope Focus. They will also be releasing many more videos and remixes of songs from the EP.
